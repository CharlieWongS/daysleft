Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Days Left is a mobile application developed by WONG NGAI CHUN. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that our app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

Data Collection

Days Left does not collect, process, or store any personal data or sensitive information from its users. The app functions entirely on your device, and no data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Days Left does not integrate with any third-party services, such as analytics tools, advertising networks, or software development kits (SDKs), that may collect data.

Data Sharing

Since Days Left does not collect any data, no data is shared with any third parties.

User Rights

As no personal data is collected, there are no data-related rights to exercise, such as data access, correction, or deletion. If you have any questions about this, please contact us.

Children’s Privacy

Days Left does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13, as no data is collected from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted within the app and on our website (if applicable), with the “Last updated” date revised accordingly.
